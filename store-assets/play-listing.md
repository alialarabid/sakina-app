## Full description (4000 char max)
Sakina is a calm companion for the daily remembrance of Allah — built to be quiet, beautiful, and free of everything that gets in the way. No ads. No tracking. No sign-up. It works fully offline.

Open it and you're greeted with salam and the Hijri date, then guided gently into your remembrance for the time of day.

What's inside:

• Morning & evening adhkar — the Adhkar as-Sabah wal-Masa, presented one at a time so you can focus, with a tap-to-count tracker for each repetition.

• Duʼa for the moments of the day — supplications for waking, leaving and entering the home, the restroom, ablution, the mosque, eating, sleeping, travel, worry, and anger.

• Tasbih counter — a simple, satisfying dhikr counter (SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu Akbar, Astaghfirullah, La ilaha illAllah) with a daily total.

• A daily moment of shukr — a gentle prompt to pause and be grateful.

• Light and dark themes, an atmosphere that shifts with the time of day, and adjustable Arabic text size.

A note on authenticity: every word of Arabic and its translation is taken verbatim from Hisn al-Muslim (Fortress of the Muslim), the well-known compilation by Saʼid bin Ali al-Qahtani. Nothing in the adhkar or duʼa is paraphrased or altered.

Sakina was made as a sadaqah jariyah — a gift, with no commercial purpose. May it benefit you and bring tranquility to your day.
