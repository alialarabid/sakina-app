Sakina — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

Sakina is a free companion for adhkar, duʼa, and dhikr. It is built as a sadaqah jariyah, with no commercial purpose. Your privacy is fully respected.

What we collect

Nothing. Sakina does not collect, store on a server, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics.

Data stored on your device

Your preferences and activity — such as theme, text size, your tasbih counts, and your progress through the adhkar — are saved only in your device’s local storage. This data never leaves your device, is not visible to us, and is removed if you clear the app’s data or uninstall it.

No ads or tracking

Sakina contains no advertising, no third-party trackers, and no tracking cookies. After it loads, the app works fully offline and makes no network requests.

Religious content

The adhkar and duʼa are taken verbatim from Hisn al-Muslim (Fortress of the Muslim) and are bundled within the app.

Children

Sakina is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated date above will change accordingly.
